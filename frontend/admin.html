How Atomic Locking Works
When a customer adds a product with stock below 10 units, the system automatically creates a 7-minute exclusive lock. This prevents other customers from purchasing the same item during checkout. If the lock expires (timer reaches 0:00), the item is automatically returned to stock. Locks are released immediately upon successful payment.
